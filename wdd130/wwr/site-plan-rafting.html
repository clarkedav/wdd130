Overview

Purpose

As the owner of the rafting business, the purpose of establishing an online presence is to enhance brand visibility, attract a broader audience, and streamline the booking process for potential customers. A well-designed website and active presence on social media platforms can serve as valuable marketing tools, showcasing the thrilling experiences offered by the rafting business, providing essential information such as trip details, safety measures, and pricing, and ultimately driving online bookings. This online presence also allows for customer engagement, reviews, and feedback, fostering a sense of community and trust among adventure seekers. Overall, the goal is to leverage digital platforms to expand the reach of the rafting business and facilitate a seamless online experience for both new and returning customers.

Audience

Our primary target audience for the water rafting website is adventurous individuals and thrill-seekers aged 18 to 40 who are seeking exciting outdoor experiences. These individuals are generally in good physical condition, enjoy outdoor activities, and have a keen interest in water sports. They are likely to have disposable income for recreational pursuits and are looking for unique, adrenaline-pumping adventures to add to their bucket list. Our secondary audience includes groups such as corporate teams, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and friends looking for memorable group activities.


Who are the target customers? Describe their life situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on). Answer: Our target customers are predominantly aged between 18 and 40, with a passion for outdoor adventures and water sports. They have a moderate to high disposable income, allowing them to indulge in recreational pursuits. Their interests include adrenaline-pumping activities, nature, and creating memorable experiences with friends.


What do they want? Answer: Our target customers are seeking thrilling and memorable water rafting experiences. They want to challenge themselves physically and mentally, enjoying the rush of navigating through rapids while surrounded by stunning natural landscapes. Additionally, they are looking for a sense of camaraderie and adventure that comes with group activities.


What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Answer: When accessing the website, our target audience expects to find comprehensive information about the rafting trips we offer, including details on difficulty levels, trip durations, safety measures, and pricing. They want to know about the scenic locations, available packages, and any special group discounts. Additionally, they expect easy navigation and a user-friendly booking platform to streamline the process of planning their adventure.
